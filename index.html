Tile and Carpet Gallery

We are a licensed and bonded contractor serving the Las Vegas area since 1992. Tile and Carpet Gallery specializes in granite, marble, porcelain, and ceramic tile installation of walls and floors. Our company can remodel kitchens, patios, bathrooms to add value to your home and we also install carpeting and wood flooring. As a locally owned and operated company serving the LV area for 20+ years, we provide excellent customer service and pride ourselves in the quality of our work. Tile and Carpet Gallery is proud to be an Angie's List certified company and we won their Super Service Award for 2016. Se Habla Espanol. We accept all major credit cards. For more information, call 702 - 630 - 8500. We accept : American Express, Discover, Visa, MasterCard, Cash or Check Serving: Las Vegas, Henderson, Boulder City, North Las Vegas, Clark County
