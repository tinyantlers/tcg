Tile and Carpet Gallery

We are a licensed and bonded contractor serving the Las Vegas area since 1992. Tile and Carpet Gallery specializes in granite, marble, porcelain, and ceramic tile installation of walls and floors. Our company can remodel kitchens, patios, bathrooms to add value to your home and we also install carpeting and wood flooring, laminate and water proof flooring with a life time guarantee. As a locally owned and operated company serving the LV area for 20+ years, we provide excellent customer service and pride ourselves in the quality of our work. Tile and Carpet Gallery is proud to be an Angie's List certified company with a five star rating and we won their Super Service Award for 2016. Se Habla Espanol. For more information, call (702) 630 - 8500. We accept all major credit cards and any form of payment. We offer free in home estimates and carry an excellent $100,000 contractor's license limit.
